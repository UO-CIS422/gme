Group Member Evaluation: Teammates


This two-page form is for evaluating each member of your team, including yourself, on contributions to the project so far. You will be asked to evaluate each member on the following attributes:

Dependable: A dependable team member makes commitments to perform some part of the work and can be depended on to finish that work competently and on time.

Constructive: A constructive team member puts the interest of the group before personal ambition and ego. A constructive team member participates in discussions in a way that helps lead toward a good outcome, rather than trying to show off or gain personal advantage. a constructive team member helps the team achieve a good result, rather than trying to obtain a result that makes the individual look good.

Engaged: An engaged team member takes an active part in the work of the group, including discussions and individual work.

Productive: A productive team member finishes a lot of work, and contributes their share of work to the group.

Asset: A member is an asset to the group if they contribute something of value. Each team member should contribute some particular skill or knowledge to the group. For example, a member might be a strong asset because of their knowledge of a web technology used by the group, or because they are skilled at managing meetings, or because they helped other members debug a critical problem.

On this page, please enter the URL of your public repository and the name of each team member. Then you will be taken to a page for entering evaluation for each member.
