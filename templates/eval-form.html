A dependable team member makes commitments to perform some part of the work and can be depended on to finish that work competently and on time.
A constructive team member puts the interest of the group before personal ambition and ego. A constructive team member participates in discussions in a way that helps lead toward a good outcome, rather than trying to show off or gain personal advantage. a constructive team member helps the team achieve a good result, rather than trying to obtain a result that makes the individual look good.
 
An engaged team member takes an active part in the work fo the group, including discussions and individual work.
 
A productive team member finishes a lot of work, and contributes their share of work to the group.
 
Each team member should contribute some particular skill or knowledge to the group. A member is an asset to the group if they contribute something of value.
